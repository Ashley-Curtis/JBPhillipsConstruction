Jeremy Phillips

I’ve been doing property management for approximately eight years. I do home inspections to help out those looking to buy, I’m not license in that field but I do have a lot of expertise to point people in the right direction At a much lower expense. I’ve been doing electrical for over 20 years, and carpentry for over 25 years.

I've been doing trade work for over 20 some years, I love working with my hands and seeing a job completed. I’ve been working for myself since approximately 2009, with very little advertising business has grown tremendously in the past three years. Word-of-mouth because a job well done means a lot. Most of my jobs come from recommendations. I’m a licensed electrician, carpenter, roofer, crack filler and painter. And what they used to call a jack of all trades and some still do, I take pride in what I do and I like to see a job well done.

I have a hidden passion, it’s writing music. I’ve written many songs, hundreds in fact. My first song that was released by a local artist became pretty popular in a couple states In a couple cities. Much to my surprise it also was in parts of Argentina and Australia. In Moncton I believe it hit number one! Ive since had two other recordings by different artists and a couple years ago I even recorded one myself. I would love to have the means to be able to record an album, Maybe someday. But I guess my main goal with music is to have a well-known artist record just one of my songs before I leave this world.
